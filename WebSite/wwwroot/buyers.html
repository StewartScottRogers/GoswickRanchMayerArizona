Zoning

RCU-2A: Rural Community Unincorporated

Goswick Ranch is zoned RCU-2A (Rural Community Unincorporated) by Yavapai County. The community's CC&Rs impose additional restrictions beyond the county minimum, ensuring the rural ranch character is preserved by covenant — not just by zoning, which can change.

County Minimum Lot Size

2 acres (RCU-2A)

CC&R Minimum Lot Size

9 acres — private covenant, harder to change than county zoning

Home Type

Site-built only. No manufactured or mobile homes permitted.

Minimum Home Size

1,500 square feet finished

Livestock & Horses

Permitted under RCU-2A Allowed Animal Chart. Equestrian use fully supported.

ADUs

One attached + one detached ADU permitted under Yavapai County's 2025 ADU ordinance (max 1,000 sq ft). CC&Rs may further restrict — verify with Board.

Realtor Information

HOA Resale Disclosure

Arizona law (ARS §33-1806) requires the HOA to provide specific disclosures within 10 business days of written notice of a sale. The process is straightforward — contact the Board directly.

What's Provided

CC&Rs, Bylaws, Rules & Regulations, current assessment amounts, HOA insurance details, reserve fund status, pending litigation (if any), and annual financial report.

Timeline

10 business days standard. 72-hour rush service available (additional $100 fee). Documents may be provided electronically or in print.

Fee Cap

Arizona law caps total resale disclosure fees at $400 aggregate ($100 for 72-hour rush; $50 update fee if 30+ days after original request).

How to Request

Submit a written request to the Board of Directors. Contact information is available on the Contact page. The Board responds promptly to all realtor inquiries.
