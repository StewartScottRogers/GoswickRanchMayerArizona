Send a Message

Get In Touch

Use this form to reach the Goswick Ranch HOA Board of Directors. The Board responds to all inquiries, typically within 3–5 business days. For time-sensitive matters, note the urgency in your message.

Common Inquiry Types

  • ARC application submission or questions
  • Document requests (CC&Rs, bylaws, financial statements)
  • HOA dues and assessment questions
  • Resale disclosure requests (realtors)
  • Gate access and community operations
  • Rule clarifications and neighbor concerns
  • Annual meeting agenda items
  • General community questions

Response Time

Standard inquiries: 3–5 business days. Document requests: within 10 business days (as required by ARS §33-1805). Urgent matters: note urgency in subject and the Board will prioritize.

Your information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

Meeting Schedule

Board & Annual Meetings

Per Arizona law (ARS §33-1804), all HOA board and member meetings are open to all community members. Board members may not prohibit members from attending, speaking, or recording meetings.

2026 Annual Meeting

Date and location to be announced. Written notice will be provided to all owners 10–50 days in advance per ARS §33-1812. Watch this page or contact the Board.

Board Meetings

Board meetings are held as needed, with 48-hour advance notice to all members. Contact the Secretary to be added to the notice list.

Member Rights at Meetings

All members have the right to attend, speak, and record any HOA meeting. The Board cannot require advance notice of recording or restrict a member's right to participate.

Quorum

Default quorum is 10% of eligible voting members (per ARS §33-1813). Your participation matters — a small community means every vote counts.
