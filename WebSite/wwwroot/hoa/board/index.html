Board of Directors

The volunteer board members who govern the Goswick Ranch HOA.

The Goswick Ranch HOA is governed by a volunteer Board of Directors elected by community residents. Board members serve staggered terms and may serve consecutive terms.

The board holds regular meetings and an annual homeowner meeting open to all residents. See Meeting Minutes for records of past meetings.

Board Member Information Pending

Names, roles, and contact information will be posted here once provided by the board.

To reach the board now, use the contact form.

Arizona law (A.R.S. § 33-1805) requires board member names and contact information to be reasonably available to all association members.
