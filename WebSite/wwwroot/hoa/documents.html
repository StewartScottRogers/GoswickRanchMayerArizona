Governing Documents

CC&Rs, bylaws, financial statements, and other official records — publicly available as required by Arizona law.

Arizona law requires the Goswick Ranch HOA to make its governing documents available to all members and prospective members. You can download them below.

These documents control what residents may and may not do with their property, how the association is governed, and the rights and responsibilities of homeownership in Goswick Ranch.

Governing Document Hierarchy

When documents conflict, higher-ranked documents control.

  1. Arizona Revised Statutes
    State law — supersedes everything. A.R.S. Title 33, Chapter 16.
  2. CC&Rs
    Recorded with Yavapai County — core property restrictions and use covenants.
  3. Bylaws
    How the HOA is governed, how elections work, and board procedures.
  4. Rules & Regulations
    Day-to-day community rules set by the board. See Rules & Regulations.

The CC&Rs are recorded instruments with Yavapai County. You can also search for them at the Yavapai County Recorder's Office.
