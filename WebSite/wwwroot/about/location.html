Directions

From Prescott via SR-69

Head east on AZ-69 (State Route 69) from Prescott through Dewey-Humboldt toward Mayer — approximately 27 miles. Once through the Mayer townsite, turn south (right) onto E Poland Rd (also shown as Poland Junction Rd). Follow Poland Rd to S Goswick Ranch Rd and turn south. The gated entrance will be on your right.

From Phoenix via I-17

Take I-17 north to Exit 262 (Cordes Junction / Mayer). Head northwest on Cordes Junction Road to AZ-69, then turn north and continue approximately 8 miles through Cordes Lakes to the Mayer area. Turn west (left) onto E Poland Rd (Poland Junction Rd). Follow Poland Rd to S Goswick Ranch Rd and look for the gated entrance.

Gate access: Goswick Ranch is a gated community. Residents are responsible for providing gate access to guests and vendors. Contact the board via the contact form for access questions.
