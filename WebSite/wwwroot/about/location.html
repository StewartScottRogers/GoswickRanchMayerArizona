Location & Directions

On E Poland Rd (off AZ-69), in the Bradshaw Mountains foothills · Mayer, AZ 86333

About Poland Road

Goswick Ranch is on E Poland Rd (also called Poland Junction Rd / CR 58 / NFSR 261), which begins at AZ-69 near the Poland Junction landmark and climbs ~5.9 miles through the Bradshaw Mountains to Five Corners at 6,926 ft. The gate is approximately 1.5 miles from AZ-69. Beyond the ranch, Poland Road continues all the way to Five Corners — the hub of the Big Bug Mesa trail and OHV network in Prescott National Forest.

Directions

From Prescott via SR-69

Head east on AZ-69 (State Route 69) from Prescott through Dewey-Humboldt toward Mayer — approximately 27 miles. Continue through the Mayer townsite on SR-69 for about 1 mile. Turn west (left) onto E Poland Rd (also signed as Poland Junction Rd). Travel approximately 1.5 miles on Poland Rd and look for the Goswick Ranch sign. The gated entrance will be on your right; gate access via lockbox.

From Phoenix via I-17

Take I-17 north to Exit 262 (Cordes Junction / Mayer). Head northwest on Cordes Junction Road to AZ-69, then turn north and continue approximately 8 miles through Cordes Lakes to the Mayer area. Continue through Mayer. Turn west (left) onto E Poland Rd (Poland Junction Rd). Travel approximately 1.5 miles and look for the gated entrance on your right.

Gate access: Goswick Ranch is a gated community using a lockbox entry system. Residents are responsible for providing gate access to guests and vendors. Real estate agents and authorized visitors obtain the key from the lockbox for showings. Contact the board via the contact form for access questions.
