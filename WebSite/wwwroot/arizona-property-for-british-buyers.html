Important Information for British Buyers

Visa and Stay Rules

UK citizens may enter the United States without a visa under the Visa Waiver Programme (ESTA) for up to 90 days per visit. For longer stays, a B-2 tourist visa may permit up to 6 months. Owning US property does not confer any US immigration rights.

For full-time retirement in the United States, a US immigration pathway would be required. Most British buyers of Goswick Ranch property will be purchasing as an investment or part-time holiday base.

Tax and Legal Considerations for British Buyers

US property ownership has specific implications for UK residents:

  • FIRPTA: 15% of the gross sale price is withheld at the time of future sale, credited against actual US tax liability.
  • US estate tax: US-sited property owned by non-US persons may be subject to US federal estate tax at death. The US–UK tax treaty provides some relief.
  • UK reporting: Foreign property holdings and any US-source income must be reported on your UK self-assessment tax return.

We strongly recommend consulting a cross-border tax solicitor or accountant before purchasing US property.

Getting to Goswick Ranch from the UK

By air: London Heathrow (LHR) and London Gatwick (LGW) have multiple daily nonstop flights to Phoenix Sky Harbor (PHX). Flight time: approximately 10–11 hours. Manchester (MAN) has seasonal nonstop service to PHX; otherwise one-stop via other US hubs. From Phoenix Sky Harbor: 74 miles north — approximately 70 minutes via I-17 N and SR 69 E.
