Utilities

Service providers for Goswick Ranch and the Mayer area.

Water

Mayer Domestic Water Improvement District (MDWID)
Provides water service to the Mayer area. Established 1993; serves the town of Mayer and sections of Poland Junction.
Address: 12994 Central Ave, Mayer, AZ 86333
Mailing: P.O. Box 416, Mayer, AZ 86333
Phone: (928) 632-4113 (office Mon–Fri 8am–noon)
After-hours / emergency: (928) 830-2747
Email: mayerwaterdistrict@gmail.com
Website: mayerwaterdistrict.com
