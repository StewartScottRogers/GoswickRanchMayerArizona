Gas & Convenience in Mayer

Several gas stations and convenience stores are on SR 69 within the Mayer/Spring Valley/Cordes Lakes corridor:

  • Gilligan's Mayer Market — 9985 S State Route 69, Mayer · (928) 632-7311 · Locally owned; regular, midgrade, premium, diesel; full grocery/C-store. The primary local option for everyday needs.
  • Circle K — 10068 S State Route 69 · (928) 632-5348
  • Gilligan's Spring Valley 76 — 17257 E Spring Valley Rd · (928) 632-4139 · AmeriGas propane dealer on-site
  • Chevron — Near I-17 at Cordes Junction · (928) 632-8558

The closest local medical facilities are along the SR 69 corridor in the Cordes Junction / Spring Valley area. For serious emergencies, see Emergency Services for hospital and medevac information.

  • Cordes Clinic / Family Medical Walk-In — 20172 E Stagecoach Trl, Ste B · (928) 632-4399 · Family medicine, psychiatric, addiction, and pediatric care; same-day and walk-in accepted; telemedicine available; accepts most insurance including Medicaid
  • Malin Medical (formerly Bradshaw Mountain Family Medicine) — 17301 E Spring Valley Rd, Ste F · (928) 632-4909 · Family practice; Mon–Thu 8:30am–5pm, Fri 8:30am–2pm
  • Cordes Pharmacy — 20172 E Stagecoach Trl, Ste A · (928) 968-5040 · Local independent pharmacy; Mon–Fri 8am–5pm; prescriptions, compounding, immunizations, durable medical equipment
  • Labcorp — 17301 E Spring Valley Rd · (928) 632-9213 · Lab/blood draw services
  • Dr. Selina West, DDS — 17301 E Spring Valley Rd, Ste C · (928) 632-9099 · Dentist — the only confirmed dental office in the 86333 ZIP code

Ranch & Equestrian Services

Goswick Ranch's 9-acre minimum lots and RCU-2A zoning accommodate horses, livestock, and working ranch operations. The following providers serve the Mayer–Prescott area:

Los Caballos Mobile Equine Veterinary Services
24/7 emergency equine veterinary care — mobile, comes to your property
Phone: (928) 636-6644

Olsen's Grain — Dewey location
Feed, hay, and ranch supplies for horses and livestock; hay delivery available
~15 miles from Goswick Ranch via AZ-69

Prescott Livestock Auction
Chino Valley, AZ (established 1988)
Regular auctions for cattle, horses, goats, and sheep; serving the entire central Arizona ranch community

Mortimer Farms
Dewey, AZ — 10–15 miles from Goswick Ranch
U-pick vegetables and fruit, seasonal festivals, agritourism — a beloved local gathering spot for the Bradshaw foothills community

Mark Goswick Cattle Co.
Bloody Basin Road area
Phone: (928) 632-7730
The continuing legacy of the Goswick family cattle ranching tradition in the valley below the subdivision.

Community Events

Mayer Daze — Mayer's signature annual festival, held on a Saturday each October (the 2024 event was the 63rd annual, meaning it began circa 1961). Location: Mayer Recreation Center area, 10001 S Wicks Ave. Free admission. Events include a parade at 10am, BBQ and bar from 11am, classic car show, karaoke, silent auction, vendor booths, carnival games (organized by Mayer High FFA), pony rides, and live entertainment. Approximately 250 attendees and 26 exhibitors.

Dewey-Humboldt Annual Labor Day Celebration — Mayer's nearest neighbor to the south holds a Labor Day parade on Main Street; open to all area residents.
