# Website Editorial Notes

## Do Not Add For-Sale Listings

**Decision (April 2026):** Do not add individual for-sale lot listings to the website.

**Reason:** Listing inventory changes too frequently to maintain reliably. Stale listings create a poor experience for buyers and reflect badly on the HOA. Realtors and buyers should be directed to MLS sources for current availability.

**What to do instead:** The `buyers.html` page covers market context (appreciation, HOA dues, tax advantages, buyer profiles, zoning) without listing specific properties. If a buyer asks about availability, direct them to contact the Board via the Contact page — the Board can refer them to current listing agents.
