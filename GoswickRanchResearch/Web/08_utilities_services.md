## Electricity

### Arizona Public Service (APS)
- **Website:** https://www.aps.com
- **Service area:** Serves most of central Arizona including the Prescott region; likely serves Mayer/Goswick Ranch area
- **Customer service:** (602) 371-3680 or (855) 688-2437 (24/7)
- **Power outage reporting / hotline:** (800) 253-9405 (24/7)
- **Outage map:** https://outagemap.aps.com/outageviewer/
- **Report outage:** https://www.aps.com/en/Utility/Outage/Outage-Center/Report-Outage
- **Start/stop service:** https://www.aps.com (residential services section)

**Note:** APS is confirmed as the largest electric utility in Arizona, serving 2.7 million customers across a territory from Douglas to the Grand Canyon, including Prescott area. Mayer is within this corridor. Confirm with board that APS is the provider (some rural areas may use a cooperative).
