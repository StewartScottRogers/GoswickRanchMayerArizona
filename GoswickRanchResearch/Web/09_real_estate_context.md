## Zoning: RCU-2A

### Overview
- **RCU** = Residential / Rural Community Unincorporated
- **2A** = Density District designating a minimum 2-acre lot (county baseline)
- Governs **unincorporated Yavapai County** (Goswick Ranch is unincorporated)
- Until an official Zoning Map is adopted by Yavapai County, all unincorporated areas default to RCU zoning

### Key RCU-2A Provisions
- **All principal and accessory uses and structures** permitted in the RCU District are allowed
- **Camping:** Permitted on 2-acre minimum lots for 10 days at a time, 3 times per year, with 30 days between stays
- **Goswick Ranch overlay:** The 9-acre minimum is a CC&R restriction (private covenant), not a county zoning rule — it is more restrictive than the county baseline

### Official Documents
- Yavapai County Planning & Zoning Ordinance (July 2025 update): https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/files/sharedassets/public/v/4/development-and-permits/permits/development-services/documents/updated-documents/amended-ord-update-7-18-25.pdf
