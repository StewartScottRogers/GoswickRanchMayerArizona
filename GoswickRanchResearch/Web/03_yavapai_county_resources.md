### Mayer Fire District

- **Website:** https://www.yavcofire.org/mayer-fire-district
- **Facilities page:** https://www.yavcofire.org/mayer-fire-facilities-stations
- **Address:** 11975 South State Route 69, Spring Valley, AZ 86333
- **Phone:** **(928) 632-9534**
- **Emergency:** 911
- **Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/MayerFireFighters/

#### Service Details
- **Type:** Combination district (paid + volunteer)
- **Services:** Fire suppression, fire prevention, wildland protection, advanced life support (ALS), rescue, extrication, and medical transportation
- **Primary service area:** ~8,000 residents across **52 square miles**
- **Extended coverage:** Additional 2,000 people across 250 square miles (total ~302 sq mi / ~10,000 residents)
- **Interstate coverage:** 25+ miles of I-17 and 13 miles of SR 69
- **Federal lands:** First responder to Prescott National Forest, BLM lands, State Lands, and Agua Fria National Monument
- **Location context:** 1 hour north of Phoenix, 30 minutes east of Prescott, 4,500 ft elevation

#### Governance
- Five-member non-partisan Board of Directors
- Board meeting notices and agendas posted publicly
- Annual fiscal budgets and reports available

#### Fire Stations
- Station 21
- Station 22
- Station 23

#### Additional
- Mayer Firefighter Charities (established 2008)
- Additional fire info: http://www.mayerfire.com/

- **Contact (Cottonwood office):** (928) 639-8151
- **Adopted building codes:** 2018 Building Codes
- Permits required for all buildings, structures, and grading in unincorporated areas

### Zoning
- **Zoning information:** https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/Development-and-Permits/Development-Services/Land-Use-Code-Enforcement-Unit/Zoning-Information
- Goswick Ranch is zoned **RCU-2A** (Rural Community Unincorporated, 2-acre minimum density district — but see note below)
- Full zoning ordinance: https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/files/sharedassets/public/v/4/development-and-permits/permits/development-services/documents/updated-documents/amended-ord-update-7-18-25.pdf
- **Note on Goswick Ranch:** The 9-acre minimum lot size is a CC&R restriction, not a county zoning requirement. The RCU-2A zoning minimum is 2 acres; the larger minimum is community-imposed.

### Property Tax Context (Mayer / Yavapai County)
- Median property tax bill in Mayer: **~$476/year** (vs. national median $2,400)
- 25th percentile: ~$257/year
- 90th percentile: ~$1,362/year
- Very low tax rates — strong selling point for the community
