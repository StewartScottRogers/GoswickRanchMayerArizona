## Key Facts Found

### Community Identity
- **Official name in real estate listings:** "Goswick Ranches" or "Goswick Ranch"
- **Type:** Exclusive gated community; described as a "new and upcoming subdivision"
- **Marketing tagline (found across multiple listing sites):** "Arizona Urban Ranch Homes"
- **Zoning:** RCU-2A (Rural Community, Unincorporated)
- **Minimum lot size:** 9 acres
- **Minimum home size:** 1,500 sq ft
- **Construction type:** Site-built only
- **Setting:** Overlooking Big Bug Creek, surrounded by the Bradshaw Mountains
- **Elevation:** Approximately 4,400–6,000 ft (community spans a range; some listings cite ~6,000 ft)
- **Access:** Gated, approximately one mile east of AZ Route 69

### Known Address / Property Examples
- **Street:** S Goswick Ranch Rd, Mayer, AZ 86333
- **Known parcel:** 5905 S Goswick Ranch Rd — single family home (1,478 sq ft, built 1949), lot 82.36 acres
- **Property range:** Listings from $250,000–$599,000 (per eXp Realty, ~5 homes for sale at time of search)
- Average listing price: ~$290,718 (eXp Realty data)
- One listing described a 20.29-acre parcel with pad options and gated access roads

### HOA Structure
- No management company found in any search result
- No HOA website found
- No professional management company listed (self-managed)
- Yavapai Title HOA list could not be accessed (SSL error); Goswick Ranch may not appear on commercial management lists given self-managed nature
