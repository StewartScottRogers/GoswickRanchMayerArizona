# Goswick Ranch HOA — Documents

Primary document repository for Goswick Ranch Homeowners Association, Mayer, Arizona (86333).

This folder contains the official legal, administrative, and cartographic documents for the property. See [CATALOG.md](CATALOG.md) for a full annotated inventory.

## Contents at a Glance

| Category | Files |
|---|---|
| Legal / Governing | CC&Rs (2 versions), Bylaws |
| Survey & Maps | Survey (PDF + PNG), Google Earth view, County map |
| Imagery | Property photo, Survey image |
| Administrative | Board of Directors contact info |

## Key Documents

- **CC&Rs** — `CC&RsGoswickHOA.pdf` is the primary HOA version; `CCRs.pdf` is the underlying recorded instrument.
- **Bylaws** — `GoswickRanchByLaws.pdf` governs HOA operations and meeting procedures.
- **Survey** — `GOSWICKRANCHSURVEY.pdf` with companion image `GoswickRanchSurveyImage.png`.
- **Board Contact** — `BoardOfDirectors.md` lists current president and contact info.

## Notes

- All PDFs should be treated as authoritative over any secondary summaries.
- Maps and imagery are for orientation; the legal survey PDF controls boundary questions.
- `BoardOfDirectors.md` may need periodic updates as board membership changes.
