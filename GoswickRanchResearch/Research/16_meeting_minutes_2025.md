## HOA vs. Road Association Discussion

- Jennifer Leitch opposed changing to a Road Association, arguing it would not benefit owners planning to build and live on their properties
- Brian Perez noted CC&Rs specify where vehicles can be parked
- Jeremy Brockman noted that a Road Association shifts enforcement to the county
- Rink Goswick asked how CC&R building requirements would be enforced under an ROA
- Ivan Gonzales noted that changing the structure would require changes to deed restrictions
- Ivan stated that converting from HOA to ROA requires **75% of property owners to agree**
- Eddie Sissons (remote) suggested creating a sub-committee to investigate pros and cons further
- Nanci Bosick noted the HOA has been at the same impasse for multiple years

**Resolution:** No change made; sub-committee to be formed to study the question further
