- **2025 rate:** $16.58 per acre per year

- **Late fee:** 10% of dues amount (imposed after Feb 1 each year)
- **Interest on late dues:** also charged (documented in ledger)
- **Total community acreage:** 891.055 acres

The previously recorded $216/year HOA fee (from a Coldwell Banker MLS listing) is consistent with $16.58 × ~13 acres = ~$215. The actual fee is **per-acre**, not flat.
