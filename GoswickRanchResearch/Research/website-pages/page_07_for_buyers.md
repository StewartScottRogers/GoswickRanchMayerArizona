## Section 1: What Is Goswick Ranch?

**H2:** What Is Goswick Ranch?

> Goswick Ranch is a private gated community on Poland Road in the foothills of Arizona's Bradshaw Mountains. Originally a working cattle ranch, the property was subdivided into approximately 25–35 parcels with a **9-acre minimum lot size** — far above the county's 2-acre baseline.
>
> The community is self-managed by a volunteer board of property owners. There is no management company.

**The fundamentals:**
- **Location:** E Poland Road, Mayer, AZ 86333 — 1.5 miles from AZ Route 69
- **Elevation:** ~4,400–6,000 ft
- **Lot minimums:** 9 acres (CC&R restriction; county minimum is 2 acres)
- **Home types:** Site-built only — no manufactured or mobile homes; 1,500 sq ft minimum
- **Gate:** Gated; physical lockbox entry
- **Roads:** Private, subdivision-maintained

**Distance from Goswick Ranch:**
| Destination | Distance | Drive |
|---|---|---|
| Mayer (groceries, gas) | ~1 mile | ~2 min |
| Prescott Valley (hospital, major retail) | ~13 miles | ~20 min |
| Prescott (downtown, airport) | ~27 miles | ~35 min |
| Phoenix Sky Harbor | ~74 miles | ~70 min |

## Section 2: HOA Fees and What They Cover

**H2:** HOA Fees

| Item | Amount |
|---|---|
| Annual HOA assessment | **$216/year ($18/month)** |
| Source | Confirmed from Coldwell Banker MLS listing (MLS# 1073459) |

> The HOA fee covers maintenance of the private internal road network and common expenses of the self-managed association. See the CC&Rs for the complete scope of HOA responsibility.

**Special assessments:**
No history of special assessments was found in public sources. Board can confirm.

**Property taxes:**
Yavapai County property taxes are among the lowest in Arizona. The median property tax on Goswick Ranch properties is approximately $476/year. One vacant-land listing (36.03 acres) showed an annual property tax of $5.00.

## Section 3: What You Can Do with Your Property

**H2:** Your Rights as a Property Owner

**Lead with what's allowed:**

> Goswick Ranch lots are designed for rural living. The CC&Rs protect the community's character — but that character is freedom and space, not restriction.

**What IS allowed:**
- Horses and equestrian facilities
- Cattle, goats, and farm animals (per county Allowed Animal Chart)
- Up to 8 chickens per lot (no roosters)
- Site-built custom homes (1,500 sq ft minimum)
- Agricultural use of the land
- One attached + one detached ADU (county standard as of Nov 2025 — CC&Rs may differ; confirm with board)
- Starlink, propane, private wells — all standard utilities

**What requires approval:**
- Any structural modification, new building, fence, or exterior change requires ARC approval before work begins

**Confirm with board:**
- Short-term rental restrictions
- Specific ADU restrictions under CC&Rs
- Commercial activity restrictions

## Section 6: Key Documents

**H2:** Key Documents for Buyers

Before purchasing, review:
- [CC&Rs (PDF)](../documents/ccrrs.pdf) — full Declaration of Covenants
- [Bylaws (PDF)](../documents/bylaws.pdf) — governance structure
- [Plat Map](https://yavapaicountyaz-web.tylerhost.net/...) — lot boundaries (Liber 56, Pages 8–12)

Arizona law (ARS §33-1806) requires the seller's agent to provide the buyer with:
- CC&Rs, Bylaws, and Rules & Regulations
- A resale disclosure certificate from the HOA
- The HOA fee, any pending special assessments, and outstanding violations

## Unique Selling Points (Summary)

For website copy and real estate agent resources:

1. **9-acre minimums** — genuine privacy; no cramped lots
2. **Gated access** — physical lockbox on Poland Road
3. **Bradshaw Mountain views + Big Bug Creek** — natural setting unsurpassed in the region
4. **~6,000 ft elevation** — escape Phoenix heat; real four seasons
5. **Extremely low property taxes** — ~$476/yr median; some land parcels as low as $5/yr
6. **Low HOA fee** — $216/year ($18/month)
7. **Accessible** — 75 mi from Phoenix, 27 mi from Prescott
8. **Equestrian access** — hundreds of miles of Prescott NF trails
9. **Bortle Class 3–4 dark skies** — Milky Way visible; no light pollution
10. **Patented land** — many lots converted from federal land via historic patent process
