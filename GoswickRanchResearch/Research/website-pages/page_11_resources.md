## Section 1: Utilities

**H2:** Utilities

### Water (Private Wells)

Each lot has an individual private well — there is no community water system.

- **Well registration / existing well lookup:** https://azwatermaps.azwater.gov/wellreg/
- **Well water testing (arsenic, lead, nitrates):** ADHR — https://www.azdhs.gov/wellwater
- **ADWR (permits, water rights):** (602) 771-8500

**Arsenic warning:** 43.6% of Yavapai County wells exceed the EPA 10 ppb arsenic limit. Annual testing is recommended given the Henrietta Mine / Big Bug Mining District proximity.

### Electricity

**Arizona Public Service (APS)**
- **Outage reporting:** (800) 253-9405
- **PSPS (Public Safety Power Shutoff) info:** https://www.aps.com/en/Utility/Outage/Public-Safety-Power-Shutoff

### Solar

The federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC) for residential solar was **eliminated January 1, 2026**. The **Arizona state solar tax credit** (25% of system cost, maximum $1,000) remains active. Contact the Arizona Department of Revenue for current eligibility.

## Section 7: Free Slash Disposal

**H2:** Free Slash Drop-Off (Fire Prevention)

Yavapai County offers free brush and slash disposal March–June at the **Mayer Transfer Station** (11250 S Antelope Creek Rd). Drop off brush, branches, and yard trimmings for free.

**Not accepted:** Roots, cacti, lumber, garbage, stumps, metal.

Program page: https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/Resident-Services/Public-Works/Solid-Waste-Division/Cleanup-and-Slash-Events/
