## Water

### Individual Private Wells (Goswick Ranch)
Goswick Ranch properties use **individual private wells** — there is no community water system. Listings confirm: "private well already on property," "well + 3,600-gallon tank."

**Well characteristics in fractured granite terrain:**
- Water is found in fracture zones; typical productive zones 130–525 ft below surface
- Rapid drainage environment; wells may respond quickly to drought conditions
- Each lot is fully responsible for its own water supply

**Regulatory framework:**
- Goswick Ranch appears to be **outside the Prescott Active Management Area (Prescott AMA)**
- Exempt domestic wells (<35 gpm) require only a Notice of Intention to Drill (NOI) filing with ADWR — no permit or ongoing monitoring
- ADWR Well Registry (look up specific wells by parcel): https://azwatermaps.azwater.gov/wellreg/
- File NOI at: ADWR, https://www.azwater.gov/

**ARSENIC WARNING — Annual well testing recommended:**
43.6% of Yavapai County groundwater samples exceed the EPA's 10 ppb arsenic limit (NCBI study). The Big Bug Mining District, which encompasses Goswick Ranch, has documented metal leaching risk. Private well owners should test every 5 years for arsenic, lead, nitrates, and mining-related metals.
- ADHS well water testing: https://www.azdhs.gov/wellwater
- Free testing available through ADHS/UA Cooperative Extension grants (check current availability)

**Note on MDWID water quality:** MDWID reports arsenic at 4.0 ppb in treated system water — below the legal limit but 1,000x above EWG's health guideline of 0.004 ppb. The district also detects total trihalomethanes (137x EWG guideline), bromoform, dibromochloromethane, haloacetic acids, and nitrate — all within federal legal limits but above health-based thresholds.

### Rainwater Harvesting
**Legal, encouraged, and supported** by Arizona state law:
- No tank size limit for residential systems; no permit required for outdoor irrigation or non-potable indoor use
- Active systems storing >10,000 gallons require ADWR permit
- **ADWR Grant Program (FY2024–2025):** Up to $2,000 reimbursement for 800+ gallon tanks ($1.00/gallon)
- At ~14–16 inches annual precipitation and a 2,500 sq ft roof, a Goswick Ranch property could yield ~15,000–20,000 gallons/year — useful for livestock, irrigation, and emergency storage
- ADWR Grants: https://water.az.gov/rural-programs/grants

### Solar Energy at Goswick Ranch

Arizona's elevation (6,000 ft) and clear skies make Goswick Ranch one of the best solar locations in the country.

| Incentive | Amount | Status |
|---|---|---|
| **Federal ITC (30%)** | **ELIMINATED** | Ended Jan 1, 2026 (signed July 4, 2025) |
| AZ State Solar Tax Credit | 25% of cost, max **$1,000** | Still active (no end date) |
| AZ Property Tax Exemption | Solar added value not taxed | Still active |
| AZ Sales Tax Exemption | 5.6% saved on equipment | Still active |

**APS Net Billing** (APS moved from true net metering to net billing in 2017):
- Export rate (Sept 2025 onward): **$0.0617/kWh** — locked for 10 years from interconnection date
- Rate has declined ~52% over 8 years; battery storage is increasingly attractive to store excess solar for self-consumption at full retail rate value (~$0.15–$0.25/kWh)
- APS Renewable Energy Riders: https://www.aps.com/en/Residential/Service-Plans/Compare-Service-Plans/Renewable-Energy-Riders

## Internet & Telecommunications

### Starlink (Satellite)
**Best option for Goswick Ranch** — confirmed available at ZIP 86333.

- Download speeds: 50–180 Mbps
- Upload speeds: 10–25 Mbps
- Latency: 20–60ms (low-Earth orbit)
- Equipment: $349 one-time (Standard Kit) or $249 (Mini) + ~$50 shipping
- Monthly plans: Residential Lite ~$80/mo; Residential MAX ~$120/mo
- Optional professional installation: ~$199
- Works well with clear southern sky view (no obstruction issues at 6,000 ft with open sightlines)
- **Assessment: Fully capable for professional remote work**
- Professional installers serving ZIP 86333: https://onsiteitsupportservices.com/arizona/mayer-az-starlink-installers-&-starlink-installation-services
