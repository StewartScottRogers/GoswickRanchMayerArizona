## 5-Year Road Improvement Plan (2024–2028)

The presentation contains two timeline slides with slightly different phasing. Slide 2 is a high-level overview; Slide 4 is the detailed project roadmap. Both are reproduced below.

---

### High-Level Timeline (Slide 2)

| Year | Scope |
|------|-------|
| **2024** | 2 culverts installed; water bars and check dams on Hawk Mountain and Vaca Bonita |
| **2025** | Enhanced security on gate entrances; new gate construction at 3 locations; possible game cameras; 1 culvert installed on Vaca Bonita; road expansion ½ mile from Vaca Bonita gate |
| **2026** | Culvert installed on Hawk Mountain Road and Vaca Bonita; Vaca Bonita entrance expanded to accommodate 18-wheelers for new construction supplies; security enhancements |
| **2027** | Hawk Mountain culvert installations; Miners Pick Road improvements including culverts and water redirections |
| **2028** | Backside of mountain — Miners Pick 1-mile stretch of 4×4-inaccessible road; repairs at Freeport and Miners Pick including Bullhorn Road |

---

### Detailed Project Roadmap (Slide 4)

**2024**
- Identification of current road conditions
- 2 projects completed: Vaca Bonita above Lot 1 and Hawk Mountain Trail

**2025**
- Prioritize projects with preference to existing and permitted home builds
- Hawk Mountain Trail entrance — road expansion
- Vaca Bonita Trail — water bar incorporation on Hawk Mountain and Vaca Bonita

**2026**
- Hawk Mountain culvert replacement (3 culverts)
- Gate replacements
- Road expansion and installation of gate opener at Vaca Bonita entrance
- Annual water bar maintenance

**2027**
- Miners Pick and Vaca Bonita Spillway work
- GeoCell installation on backside of mountain
- Backside Miners Pick culverts and road reinforcement using GeoCell
- Annual water bar maintenance

**2028**
- Continue backside of mountain work using GeoCell
- Road plowing
- Annual water bar maintenance

## Road Map (Slide 3)

The presentation includes a parcel map of the ranch with three types of markers:

- **Green triangles** — Culvert installations completed
- **Red triangles** — Culverts needed
- **Cyan X** — Road expansion locations

Roads named on map: Hawk Mountain Road, Vaca Bonita Trail, Freeport Road, Miners Pick Road, Bullhorn Road, Maude Mule Trail, Gallina Lane. Completed culverts appear near the Vaca Bonita entrance area and lower portions of the ranch; needed culverts are concentrated along Hawk Mountain Road and upper/backside roads.

---

## Materials and Resources (Slide 5)

- **Backhoe** — key resource for culvert installation and road expansion
- **Culverts** — 24 culverts, 20" diameter, to be utilized across the ranch
- **GeoCell** — aggregate retention and erosion control on road surfaces
- **Water bars** — installed along existing road surfaces for drainage
- **Labor** — all work currently volunteer-based; hiring of paid operators may be needed to meet timeline
- **Document migration** — all physical documents being migrated to Google Drive (in progress); presentation link to be shared with owners who have provided email addresses
