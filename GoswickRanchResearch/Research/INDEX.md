## Key Findings Across All Research

### High-Confidence Facts

- **HOA fee:** $216/year (confirmed from Coldwell Banker MLS listing)
- **Zoning:** RCU-2A (Rural Community Unincorporated); 9-acre minimum is CC&R restriction, not county zoning (county minimum is 2 acres)
- **Plat recording:** Goswick Ranch LS 56/8-12 (Liber 56, Pages 8–12, Yavapai County Recorder)
- **Gate:** Physical lockbox entry (not electronic); internal roads are private
- **Water:** Individual private wells — NOT Mayer Domestic Water Improvement District
- **Electric:** Arizona Public Service (APS) — confirmed
- **Henrietta Mine:** USGS MRDS #10027183; within/adjacent to Goswick Ranch; 18 active mining claims; ADEQ air quality permit 2022; reclamation proceedings 2023; arsenic leaching risk
- **Arsenic warning:** 43.6% of Yavapai County wells exceed EPA 10 ppb limit; Big Bug Mining District is a documented source of metal leaching — annual testing recommended
- **Federal solar ITC:** ELIMINATED January 1, 2026; AZ state 25% credit (max $1,000) still active
- **2017 Goodwin Fire/flood:** 28,516 acres; Big Bug Creek flooded July 19, 2017; 109 homes damaged; hydrophobic soil mechanism
- **Lynx Lake:** Intentionally drained late 2024 for dam valve repairs; refilling slowly; boat ramp closed as of March 2026

### Important Caveats

- **Goswick family genealogy:** Confirmed presence in 1930 Mayer census (George Allen Goswick); Mark Goswick Cattle Co still active. Specific connection between the family and subdivision naming is inferred, not documented.
- **Phone numbers and contacts:** All county/emergency contacts confirmed from multiple sources but verify before publishing on website
- **Real estate prices:** Active listings from March 2026; prices change frequently; use for context, not as current market data
- **2025 Arizona HOA legislation:** All bills described (SB 1039, SB 1494, SB 1337) have effective dates of September 26, 2025
