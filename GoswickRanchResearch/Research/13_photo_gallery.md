## Overview

21 photos from PAAR MLS (Prescott Area Association of Realtors) listings for Goswick Ranch and adjacent parcels, spanning 2022–2026. All photos are in `GoswickRanchResearch/PhotoGallery/` organized into five category subfolders.

All photos carry `©PAAR MLS` watermarks and are sourced from MLS listings. Do not republish on the HOA website without verifying usage rights with PAAR or the listing agent.
