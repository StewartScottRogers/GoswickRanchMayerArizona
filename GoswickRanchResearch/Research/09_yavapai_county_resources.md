### Mayer Fire District

- **Website:** https://www.yavcofire.org/mayer-fire-district
- **Facilities page:** https://www.yavcofire.org/mayer-fire-facilities-stations
- **Address:** 11975 South State Route 69, Spring Valley, AZ 86333
- **Phone:** **(928) 632-9534**
- **Emergency:** 911
- **Facebook:** https://www.facebook.com/MayerFireFighters/

#### Service Details
- **Type:** Combination district (paid + volunteer)
- **Services:** Fire suppression, fire prevention, wildland protection, Advanced Life Support (ALS), rescue, extrication, and medical transportation
- **Primary service area:** ~52 square miles, ~8,000 residents
- **Extended coverage:** ~302 sq miles total, ~10,000 residents
- **Coverage:** 25 miles of I-17; 13 miles of SR 69; Prescott National Forest, BLM, State Lands, Agua Fria National Monument
- **Elevation:** approximately 4,500 feet

#### Fire Stations
- Station 21 — (928) 910-2045; Type 1 engine; constantly staffed; burn permits issued
- Station 22
- Station 23

#### Additional Resources
- Mayer Firefighter Charities (established 2008)
- Additional fire info: http://www.mayerfire.com/

- **Contact (Cottonwood office):** (928) 639-8151
- **Adopted building codes:** 2018 Building Codes
- Permits required for all buildings, structures, and grading in unincorporated areas

### Zoning
- **Zoning information:** https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/Development-and-Permits/Development-Services/Land-Use-Code-Enforcement-Unit/Zoning-Information
- Goswick Ranch is zoned **RCU-2A** (Rural Community Unincorporated)
- Full zoning ordinance (July 2025 update): https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/files/sharedassets/public/v/4/development-and-permits/permits/development-services/documents/updated-documents/amended-ord-update-7-18-25.pdf
- Use Districts chapter: https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/files/sharedassets/public/v/1/development-and-permits/development-services/documents/usedistricts.pdf
- Planning FAQ (2025): https://www.yavapaiaz.gov/files/sharedassets/public/v/4/development-and-permits/development-services/documents/planning-faq-2025.pdf
- **Note:** The 9-acre minimum lot size is a CC&R restriction, not a county zoning requirement. The RCU-2A county minimum is 2 acres.

### Ordinances & Regulations

### Property Tax Context (Mayer / Yavapai County)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Mayer median property tax | ~$476/year |
| 25th percentile | ~$257/year |
| 90th percentile | ~$1,362/year |
| National median | ~$2,400/year |
| Yavapai County effective rate | ~0.44% |

Strong selling point for the community — property taxes are dramatically lower than national median.
